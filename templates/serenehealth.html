Puri Anyar Kerambitan Tour

Puri Anyar Kerambitan is a historical site located in the village of Kerambitan in Tabanan Regency, Bali, Indonesia. "Puri" translates to "palace" in Indonesian, and Puri Anyar Kerambitan refers to the royal palace complex in Kerambitan. This palace holds significant cultural and historical importance in Bali, as it was once the residence of the royal family of Kerambitan. The palace showcases traditional Balinese architecture, intricate designs, and cultural artifacts that reflect the island's rich heritage.
